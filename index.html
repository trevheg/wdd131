San Jose, California

San Jose is the largest city in Northern California, the third-largest city in California, and the thirteenth-largest city in the United States of America. It is the unofficial "Capital of Silicon Valley," with local companies including Google, Meta, eBay, Nvidia, Intel, AMD, and Netflix. At least one tour guide has said that while it hosts some interesting museums, it is most notable as being close to more interesting destinations such as San Francisco, Santa Cruz, and Monterrey.
